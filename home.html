Our story

IT OWL, LLC started as a result of the experience of the owner coming to the United States with her family due to her spouse's IT career.

IT OWL's hope is to give back to U.S. companies, by finding reliable IT professionals that help them grow and keep evolving in the area of Information Technology, and to pave the way for other professionals through a positive personal experience with steps toward working for a U.S.-based company, on-site or remotely.

TEAM
